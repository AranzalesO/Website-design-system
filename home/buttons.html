Buttons

Buttons in web design are interactive elements that allow users to perform specific actions, such as submitting a form, navigating to another page, or triggering a function. They are essential for user engagement and navigation, often appearing as clickable elements with distinct visual styles to grab attention. Buttons are designed to be easily identifiable through their labels or icons, guiding users on how to interact with a website. Effective button design includes clear text or icons, contrasting colors, and responsive behavior to provide a seamless and intuitive user experience.
