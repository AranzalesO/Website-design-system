Fonts

The word font refers to a set of printable or displayable typography or text characters in a specific style and size. Font styles are used in both print and digital text. There are five basic classifications of typefaces: serif, sans serif, script, monospaced, and display.

As a general rule, serif and sans serif typefaces are used for either body copy or headlines (including titles, logos, etc.), while script and display typefaces are only used for headlines.
